Are you looking for a little break on your journey? Then come stop by the enchanted little garden soon!

On July 25, 2026, starting at noon, there will be all sorts of curious things to explore there. Until the next morning, you can expect wonderful sounds and art, a drink or two, dancing dreams, caring friends, and a cozy place to stop by and take your time.

So come anytime (just please RSVP beforehand!)

QUICK FACTS

Join our What'sApp group chat for updates and any new information!

When and Where?
On July 25, 2026, starting at 1:30 PM in an enchanted garden between Steinbachtal and Höchberg (Coordinates: 49°46'30.8"N 9°53'27.9"E Google Maps)

Admission:
From 1:30 PM until the end (registered people only)

Donation:
Suggested donation: 15-25 euros

Music:
From 2:00 p.m to 9:30 a.m.

IMPORTANT

Make sure to bring cash, as we cannot accept card payments!

PROGRAM

There will be two stages: the Queerbeet and the Teestube

The Queerbeet will feature a colorful mix of music from 2:00 p.m. to 9:30 a.m. We’ve made sure to include something for every taste. You can find the lineup here: Timetable

The Teestube will also offer a fine and curious selection: Expect musical treats, cozy and atmospheric experiences, as well as live performances and more experimental sounds to discover.
We’re still busy selecting and organizing the program, so let yourself be surprised!

There will also be workshops. You can find the respective topics and times here: Workshops

In addition, you can look forward to all sorts of cozy spots to linger, take your time, and simply sit back, as well as a “Treibgut” clothing swap corner.

FOOD & DRINKS

Drinks:
There will be free drinking water available to refill your bottles, but please remember to bring your own bottle or container!

At the Bar “Minimum”, you can buy drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) for a small fee. Be sure to bring cash!!

We ask that you support the festival and not bring too many drinks yourself, since we’ll be offering drinks at a very fair price.

Food:
For a small fee, you can get a vegan meal from us to refuel.

You’re also welcome to bring your own food, and we’d of course love it if you brought a snack or two to share!

HYGIENE

Restrooms:
Two restrooms on the grounds are unisex. Anyone is welcome to use them.

Another restroom is a “FLINTA*” restroom, equipped with hygiene products such as tampons. This is a composting toilet. Instructions for use are posted on the right-hand wall!

Please treat the restrooms with care, look out for one another, and keep them clean. After all, we all want the restroom facilities to remain pleasant and acceptable for everyone, even the next morning!

Shower:
There’s also a shower on the grounds in case you need to cool off during the event. It’s not lockable and is visible from the outside, so you might want to bring a swimsuit if you need a little more privacy!
