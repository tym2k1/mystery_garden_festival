The children of Mama Morgana, together with the collectives TEOS, Exhaust the Emptiness, and Computerklang, want to create a space for art, culture, and subculture.
A space where there is time to get lost and confused, to find your place and simply be.

A space where everyone can exist, a space full of curious characters in beautiful garments, filled with enchanting curiosities and playful creations.
We invite you all to join us and be part of it.

We want to celebrate diversity, creativity, and consideration in this space, and we are happy about every extraordinary outfit and every funny little thing you bring along.

One thing is clear to us: racism, antisemitism, sexism, and other forms of dehumanizing attitudes have no place here.
