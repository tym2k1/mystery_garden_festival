There are only a limited number of spots in the garden, so don’t wait too long!

Registration opens on July 21, 2026, but we’ll also let you know in the group chat when it starts.


You can sign up here for the festival as well as for supporting shifts (door staff, bar, awareness) during the festival to help us out. (activation at 21.07).


Please be as committed as possible, since we have limited spots and would really like to fill them!

The entire festival is funded by donations. Of course, every little bit helps make it affordable, but if you’re able, we’d ask you to contribute a donation of 15-25 euros.
We know that’s more than a typical night out at a club, but in return, we can offer you even more to listen to and discover!

If you can’t afford that but would still like to come, please reach out to @wartechlange on Instagram — maybe we can find a solution.
